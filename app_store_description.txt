Discover the wonders of ancient Egypt with your personal travel companion! Ancient Tour Guidance transforms your Egyptian adventure into an exciting journey through history.

Explore famous places like the Great Pyramid, Sphinx, and Egyptian Museum with detailed information and location guides. Navigate to historical sites with integrated Apple Maps. Track your visited places and monitor your trip progress with a smart checklist.

Spin the Fortune Wheel daily for mystical quests and rewards from ancient Egyptian gods. Discover and collect 20 legendary Egyptian artifacts with rich mythology and lore. Journey through time and explore key historical events at each location with the Time Portal feature.

Stay informed with essential scam warnings and prevention tips. Save your preferred destinations for quick access. Capture and store photos of your experiences.

Perfect for tourists, history enthusiasts, and adventure seekers visiting Egypt. Turn your trip into an unforgettable gamified experience!

No ads. No in-app purchases. Just pure exploration and fun.
